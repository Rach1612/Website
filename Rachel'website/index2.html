About Hermitage Green

Hermitage Green are an Irish (Limerick based) folk rock band with a traditional flavour. The band now consists of 5 players, each of which had very different careers before starting the band. While they did not decide on being a band they had just came to the end of their different careers and it all came together for them. Their first album was called "save your soul" and they have played countless live concerts and gigs in bar to date.
