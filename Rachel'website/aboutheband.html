Barry Murphy was a founding member of the band, after a sucessful career playing professional rugby Barry moved into the music scene.

Dan murphy is lead singer of the band, plays guitar and harmonia. Before Hermitage Green he trying to get into Radio production.

Darragh Graham plays drums, guitar, and was a strength and conditioning coach before joining the band

Darragh Giffen writes a lot of the lyrics for Hermitage Green songs, plays piano and sings. He was a primary school teacher before joining the group

Dermot Sheedy plays percussion, bodhan and drums.He had just finished college but had been gigging since he was 17.
